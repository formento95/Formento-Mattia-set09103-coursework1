Four Iconic Guitar Models




Gibson ES-150


The ES-150 was the first commercially successful electric guitar. It achieved unprecedented notoriety due in large part to its endorsement by promiment guitar players of the day such as Eddie Durham Floyd Smith and Charlie Christian.



Fender Telecaster


The first commercially successful solid-body electric guitar. Simple yet effective design and bright single-coil sound revolutionized electric guitar manufacturing and popular music.



Gibson Les Paul


This instrument was the first solid-body electric guitar sold by the Gibson Guitar Corporation.Initially something of a commercial failure, however was saved by bluesman like Mike Bloomfield and British rockers like Keith Richards, Eric Clapton and Jimmy Page.



Gretsch 6128 Duo Jet (1953)


When Fender and Gibson Started selling solid-body guitars the Gretsch Company introduced the Duo Jet, arguably the first true rock and roll axe.
