Four Iconic Bass Guitar Models




Hofner 500/1 Violin Bass


This Bass guitar was designed by Walter Hofner in 1955. His intention was to produce a bass guitar that would be appealing to players of the bass (double bass) who no longer wanted to carry such a big heavy instrument. The Hofner 500/1 is also the bass guitar Paul McCartney used to play with the Beatles.



Fender Precision Bass


The Precision Bass through its many incarnations is and will always be the industry standard and safe choice for any application. The bass was so popular that you would see ads that read "Band Looking for Fender Bass Player".



Fender Jazz Bass


The Jazz Bass was introduced in 1960 as an alternative to the Precision Bass. Though it was a sibling of the Precision Bass it had many key diffrences. The result was a rich brighter tone with more pronounced mid and upper frequencies.



Musicman StingRay


This was a very innovative bass. It was wired with active circuitry, via a 9V battery. This gave the bass unprecedented punch and power.
